Informations about our site:

What is the DOAR Project ?

               The DOAR (portuguese for donate) project is a platform where charity institutions can receive donations or 
        connect to voluntary workers,

Why the platform was created ?

               The Doar platform is a project created to facilitate and act as mediator for donations between people and charity instituions. You
	could, for example, search for institutions doing work with homeless people in your city and volunteer or make a money donation. How? 
        In the DONATE tab in the superior section of our site you can look for instituions, read a brief description and choose 
	the one that better suits your aims. Clicking on the instituion profile will redirect you to a more detailed page
        where you can look for more information e make a donatio.

What which tab does??

  • Home
    •  This our homepage, where you'll be when you access the site for time. It contains some information about us.
  • Donate
    • Where you can look for institutions and choose one to make a donation.
  • About
    • This is where you are now. Here we have more detailed information about the project and how the platform works.
  • Sign in
    • Where you connect to your account as a donator or institution.
  • Sign Up
    • Where you can create your account as a instution or as a donator/voluntary worker.
